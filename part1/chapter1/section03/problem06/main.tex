\problem{}\label{s3p6}

Define a relation on the plane by setting \[({x_0},{y_0})<({x_1},{y_1})\] if
either \({y_0}-{{x_0}^2}<{y_1}-{{x_1}^2}\), or
\({y_0}-{{x_0}^2}={y_1}-{{x_1}^2}\) and \({x_0}<{x_1}\). Show that this is an
order relation on the plane, and describe it geometrically.
