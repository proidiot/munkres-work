\subproblem{}\label{4a}
For every \(a \in A\), it is true that \(a^2 \in B\).

\begin{remark}[Negation of~\ref{4a}]
	The statement
	\[\exists a \in A: a^2 \notin B\]
	is the negation of the statement
	\[\forall a \in A: a^2 \in B\]
\end{remark}
