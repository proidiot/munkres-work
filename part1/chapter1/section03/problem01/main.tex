\problem{}\label{s3p1}

Define two points \((x_0,y_0)\) and \((x_1,y_1)\) of the plane to be equivalent
if \(y_0 - {x_0}^2 = y_1 - {x_1}^2\). Check that this is an equivalence relation
and describe the equivalence classes.
